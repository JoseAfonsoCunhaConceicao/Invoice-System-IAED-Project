◆ rotateLeft()

TreeNode * rotateLeft ( TreeNode * node)

Performs a simple left rotation.

COMPLEX LOGIC EXPLANATION (AVL Rotations): The following rotation functions (Left, Right, Right-Left, Left-Right) are used to restore the O(log n) tree balance after insertions/deletions. They execute a local restructuring of pointers to shift the subtree's center of gravity without violating the binary search structural property (Left children remain < Root, Right children remain > Root).

Parameters
nodeRoot of the unbalanced structure.
Returns
Meaningful new root of the subtree after rotation.
  1. Perform rotation: pull right child up, push current node down
  1. Re-calculate heights for the affected nodes
